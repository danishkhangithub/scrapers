China Petroleum & Chemical Corporation (Hong Kong SAR, China)

Main Activities: Crude Petroleum Extraction | Petroleum Refineries | Petrochemical Manufacturing
Secondary Activities: Petroleum and Petroleum Products Merchant Wholesalers | Gasoline Stations
Full name: China Petroleum & Chemical Corporation Profile Updated: April 06, 2021
Buy our report for this company USD 29.95 Most recent financial data: 2020 Available in: English & Chinese Download a sample report

China Petroleum & Chemical Corporation is an energy and chemical company that is primarily engaged in oil and gas and chemical operations in the PRC. Oil and gas operations consist of exploring for, developing and producing crude oil and natural gas; transporting crude oil and natural gas by pipelines; refining crude oil into finished petroleum products; and marketing crude oil, natural gas and refined petroleum products. Chemical operations include the manufacture and marketing of a wide range of chemicals for industrial uses. The Company was incorporated in 2000 and is headquartered in Beijing, China.
